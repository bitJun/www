Returning Units

If it has been determined that the product will need to be repaired at one of our authorized facilities, an RMA number will be issued (Return Material Authorization number). This number should be clearly visible on all shipment documents. Please note, returned goods will not be accepted without an official RMA number.

When possible, please ship the product back in its original packaging. We can not accept liability for products damaged during shipping back to us.

Upon reception of your product, preliminary assessment will be performed. Products damaged through neglect due to inadequate packaging or during transit, will have the warranty voided. All of our products require stable and industry standard packing materials.

IMPORTANT

1. When returning items to DVB-TECH, please indicate on the commercial invoice and shipping documents “It- alian Goods Returning to Manufacturer for Repair”, along with the RMA # provided as reference and the val- ue for customs purposes.

REPAIR COSTS

Warranty Repair: Units which are under warranty are of no charge to the customer. In the event that there is “No Fault Found”, a charge of EURO €2,500.00 per unit will be invoiced to cover evaluation, labor and testing, prior to shipping.

As per our Warranty procedures, the Customer is responsible for shipment to DVB-TECH and DVB-TECH is responsible for the return shipment.
