What payment methods are available?

Currently, we accept PayPal,Credit Card and Wire Transfer payment methods.

Paypal

Redirect to PayPal via a secure online connection, and pay with the balance of your PayPal account or Credit/Debit

Credit/Debit Card

Pay online with Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and UnionPay Card. 

Prepaid Wire Transfer

JLCPCB accepts direct wire transfer from your bank to ours. You can pre-transfer money into our bank account, and the fund will be added to your JLCPCB Payment balance, then you can use the balance to pay for orders on JLCPCB.com. 

Please contact us at support@jlcpcb.com if you have any questions.
